### Chandler's room / Ross, Joey and Chandler are talking in the room

```
Ross : I'm divorced! I'm only 26 and I'm divorced!

Joey : Shut up!

Chan : You must stop!

Ross : What if there's only one woman for everybody? I mean, what if you get one woman and that's it? Unfortunately in my case, there was only one woman for her...
Joey : What are you talking about? 'One woman'? That's like saying there's only one flavor of ice cream for you. C'mon, there's lots of flavors out there.

Ross : I am here to take comfort. but, now, I don't know, if I'm hungry or horny.

Chan : Stay out of my freezer!
```

#### Vocabulary & Idiom
```
- flavor 맛, 향미, 풍미
- C'mon = Come on 
    - 제발 좀 그만해 = stop it / drop it / enough / knok it off
    -  망설이지 말고 제발 해 please / for god's sake
- Stay out of ~에서 피하다, 떨어지다
- horny (informal) 성적으로 흥분한, 여자가 고픈
- to take comfort 위로 받기 위해
- That's like saying S+V 그건 ~ 라고 말하는것과 같은거야.
- What if ~면 어쩌지?
- I mean 말하자면...
- What if you get one woman and that's is? 한명의 여자와 만나고 그게 끝이면? (다라면?)
- get a trim 머리를 다듬다, 손질하다.
- make a deposit 은행 계좌 만들다
```

#### Pronunciation
```
- I'm only 26 and I'm divorced! (d 발음은 생략)
- You must stop. (s 다음 t 발음은 생략)
```

#### Pattern
```
나 ~ 하러 왔어
- I'm here to help you
- I'm here to stay with you.
- I'm here to see your boss.
- I'm here to get a trim.
- I'm here to make a deposit.
- I'm here to meet my friend.
- I'm here to go abroad.
```
