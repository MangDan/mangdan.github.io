### Monica's room / Monica is cleaning up her room

```
Mon : Are you through with that?

Joey : Yeah, sorry, the swallowing slowed me down.

Mon : Whose little ball of paper is this?

Chan : Oh, uh, that would be mine. See, I wrote a note to myself, and then I realized I didn't need it, so I balled it up and... now I wish I was dead.

Mon : Looou do this every time they come. The place looks great.
```

#### Vocabulary & Idiom
```
- through with through는 처음부터 끝까지 관통하는 의미로 끝내다라는 의미
- Swallowing slowed me down 천천히 음미하면서 삼키는(마시는)
- Whose little ball of paper 누구의 종이 뭉치
- Would be mine Would는 미래의 가정으로 쓰임
    - That would be mine 아마 내거일거야
    - He would come here 그가 아마 여기로 올거야
- Would you relax? 안정좀 해줄래? 그만좀 해줄래?
- I realized 나는 이제 알았어 (know는 이미 알고 있는거)
- balled it up 종이를 구겼어 (동그랗게...)
```

#### Pronunciation
```
- Are ou through with that? (with의 th는 생략)
- I just(t는 발음하지 않음) don't(t는 발음하지 않음) wanna push you.
```

#### Pattern
```
I'm in 나는 ~한 상태야, 상황이야
- I'm in a hurry. 서둘러야 해
- I'm in trouble 곤경에 처했어
- I'm in love with her. 사랑에 빠졌어.
- I'm in my early thirties. 나는 30대 초반이야
- I'm in the middle of saying something. 먼가 말하고 있는 중이야
- I'm in charge of the sales department. 나는 영업부를 책임지고 있어.
```
